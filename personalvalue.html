My Personal Values

What guides me in life

  • Self discipline
  • Accountability
  • Hard work
  • Respect
class="Self discipline"

Self discipline is something that I strongly believe that is important to have in everyday life because it helps you stay focused and make the right choices even when it is difficult or tempting to take shortcuts.

class="Accountability"

Accountability is another important thing that you need in everyday life because it means that you are taking responsibility for your actions, which also helps build trust and helps you grow from your mistakes.

class="Hard work"

Hard work is another major piece to everyday life because it helps reach your goals, and helps build a great mindset.

class="Respect"

Respect is the last key piece in everyday life because it helps you build great relationships and builds a good enviroment where people will feel like people and not objects.
