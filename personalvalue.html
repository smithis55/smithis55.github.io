My Personal Values

Values Industry

My main components of life are: Self discipline, Accountability, Hard work, and Respect. In my opinion if you have these four values you will be okay in life.

Self discipline

  1. Self discipline to me means controlling my actions, staying focused and doing what is right even when I don't want to.
  2. Growing up I was taught self discipline through out school, sports, and learning consistency when I had goals to reach.
  3. Self discipline is important to me because it helps me stay consistent in everything I do.
  4. If I didn't have self discipline, I would've fell into bad things and it would've stopped me from reaching my goals.

Accountability

  1. Accountability to me means taking responsibility in your actions and owning up to things that you did good or bad.
  2. Accountability was installed in me at a young age with me growing up and getting in trouble, playing sports and real life events.
  3. Accountability is important to me because it helps you grow as a person and helps you earn respect.
  4. Without accountability I would blame others for my actions and wouldn't grow as a person.

Hard work

  1. Hard work to me means working consistently and giving effort to something that you really want and not giving up when things get hard.
  2. Hard work came from me watching my parents, playing sports, and school because I always wanted more for myself.
  3. Hard work is important because without hard work you wouldn't get nowhere in life and it also helps you stand out from others.
  4. Without hard work I would not be at the University of South Carolina or have anything I have in my life right now.

Respect

  1. Respect to me means treating others how you would want to be treated.
  2. Respect was another thing installed in me at a young age by my parents.
  3. Respect is important because it helps build good relationships with people and helps build positive enviroments.
  4. Without respect you won't get many things in today's world because people will not want to talk to you.
